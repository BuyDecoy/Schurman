What's Next?*


So, what's next? In terms of website design, I think it will stay more of a hobby. After exploring it, I have found it very interesting and enjoyable to work on but when I think about doing it as a career with timelines and with standards to have to follow, it begins to seem like it's not the job for me. Coding this website has allowed me to realize and cement the fact that I really do have a love for technology so I will continue to explore my options.
